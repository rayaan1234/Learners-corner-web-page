Who are We?

Learner’s Corner Guidance is the ultimate learning destination for all 4th Graders. Our highly experienced teachers are here to widen the horizons of your child in any subject they require assistance with. These tutors are well-known for their highly interactive and hands-on teaching methods that will allow your child to understand the fundamentals of each subject, providing them with a solid foundation that will hold forth for a lifetime of better learning. Pupils benefit from the use of specially designed interactive computer software, worksheets and resource books.

As a parent, you may very well ask, why start so early? But at Learner‘s Corner, we believe that starting early is the KEY to a lifetime of better learning. We aim to make the children under our guidance self-sufficient and independent, who will gradually acquire the tools to take control of their own learning and become better self-learners. This is what we preach in our motto of Learning How to Learn and this is our steadfast promise to our parents.

Guidelines

1. Now that you have attended our webinar and understood the key features of our services, we invite you to complete the below form to register your interest to begin your child’s journey of Learning How to Learn. The form will be open from Sunday, 11.00 am IST for a limited time of 48 hours following our webinar.



2. After completion of the form, our team will contact you within 24 hours to acquire more details about the specific requirements of your ward.

3. They will offer a free student assessment to determine the level at which your child currently works and then advise the most appropriate course of action for your child.

4. Based on the information provided by you, we shall assign a tutor tailored to your child’s needs for a Demo Session of 1 hour. This service can be availed for multiple subjects, each with a different tutor proficient in the subject.

5. Please note that to avoid misuse of our services by dishonest actors, we are charging a nominal fee of 100 INR per demo session per tutor. This fee is fully refundable should you choose to enroll your ward with us and will be adjusted against the payment towards our services.

6. The link for payment towards the Demo session and all further classes will be generated by the customer service representative.

7. On joining with us, you will be assigned a dedicated Student Support Counsellor who will always be available to advice and help you.

8. For any clarifications regarding the above, please feel free to contact customersupport@learnercorner.com.
